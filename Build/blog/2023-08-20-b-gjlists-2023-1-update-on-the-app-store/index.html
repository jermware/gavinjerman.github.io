gjLists Updated on the App Store

August 20, 2023
Tagged with: gjlists, ios, app, app store
128 words; 1 minutes to read.

gjLists has been updated to v2023.1 on the App Store.

This is a maintenance release with the following changes:

  • Fixed a bug that could cause the app to exit unexpectedly when loading lists from iCloud.
  • Replaced the deprecated CountAPI with TelemetryDeck for the collection of anonymous usage data for private analytics to help understand how the app is used and to support future developments.
